## Overview

Bespoke Woodcraft Studio runs primarily on inbound sales driven by our own content, referrals from existing clients, and presence in woodworking/maker communities. The typical sales cycle is 2-4 weeks from first conversation to signed retainer. Deal sizes range from $2,000-$8,000/month depending on service scope. Outbound is limited and targeted — we reach out to makers and brands we genuinely admire and want to work with.

---

## Sales Stages

| Stage | Activities | Exit Criteria | Typical Duration |
|-------|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Lead | Inbound inquiry via website, DM, or referral; initial info captured | Contact info and basic project details collected | 1-2 days |
| Qualified | Review against ICP; confirm budget range, content needs, and timeline | Meets ICP criteria; budget aligns with our packages | 2-3 days |
| Discovery Call | 30-minute video call to understand their business, goals, current content, and pain points | Clear understanding of scope; mutual interest confirmed | 1 week |
| Proposal | Custom proposal with recommended package, pricing, timeline, and example work | Proposal sent and reviewed by client | 3-5 days |
| Negotiation | Handle objections, adjust scope or pricing, finalize terms | Agreement on scope, pricing, and start date | 3-7 days |
| Closed Won | Contract signed, deposit received, kickoff scheduled | Signed agreement and payment received | — |

## Qualification

We use the ICP criteria in [ideal-customer-profile.md](../business/ideal-customer-profile.md) to qualify prospects.

**Quick qualification checklist:**
- [ ] Is this a woodworker, maker, or maker-adjacent brand with a real product or service?
- [ ] Do they have a budget of at least $2,000/month for content services?
- [ ] Are they committed to showing up on camera or providing access to their shop/process?
- [ ] Do they have a clear business goal that content can support (more clients, audience growth, product launch)?

---

## Discovery Call Structure

1. **Open with context:** Thank them for reaching out, confirm how they found us, and set the agenda for the call (30 minutes, learn about them, share how we work, discuss fit).
2. **Understand their world:** What do they make? Who are their customers? What does their current content look like? What's working and what's not? What would success look like in 6 months?
3. **Share our approach:** Walk through how we work — monthly cycles, batch shooting, the content pipeline from shoot to publish. Show 1-2 relevant examples from similar clients.
4. **Close for next steps:** If there's mutual fit, propose sending a custom package and pricing within 3 business days. Schedule a follow-up call to review the proposal together.

---

## Pricing

Pricing is package-based with three standard tiers (Starter, Growth, Full Service), each defined by the number of platforms managed, content volume, and whether video production is included. Custom quotes are available for larger brands or non-standard scopes. All retainers are month-to-month after an initial 3-month commitment. One-off project pricing (e.g., a single brand video) is quoted per project.

---

## Handoff to Delivery

When a deal closes:

1. Sales sends the signed contract, client brief, and discovery call notes to the production team via Notion
2. Production lead schedules a client kickoff call within 5 business days to align on the first month's editorial calendar
3. Client is added to shared Google Drive, Frame.io workspace, and communication channels (Slack or email thread)

See [Client Onboarding Template](../delivery/client-onboarding-template.md) for the full onboarding process.
