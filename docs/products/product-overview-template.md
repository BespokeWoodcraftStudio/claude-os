## What It Is

Bespoke Woodcraft Studio provides end-to-end content creation services for woodworkers and makers who want to grow their businesses. We produce video content, manage social media channels, and develop brand content strategies that turn skilled craftspeople into recognized voices in the maker community. Our services solve the core problem most woodworkers face: they're exceptional at their craft but lack the time, tools, or know-how to market themselves effectively online.

---

## Who It's For

**Target audience:** Independent woodworkers, custom furniture makers, and maker-brand founders who want to grow their audience and revenue through content.

**Use cases:**
- A custom furniture builder who wants a YouTube channel that drives client inquiries
- A tool or supply brand targeting the woodworking community that needs authentic, high-quality social content
- A maker launching a course or product line who needs video production and a content rollout strategy

---

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Video Production | Full-service shop video — filming, editing, sound design, and publishing for YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok |
| Social Media Management | Daily posting, community engagement, and growth strategy across platforms |
| Brand Content Strategy | Positioning, messaging, and editorial calendars tailored to the maker/woodworking niche |
| Content Repurposing | Turning one long-form shoot into short clips, reels, stills, and written posts to maximize reach |

---

## Positioning

**Category:** Content creation agency for the trades and maker economy

**One-liner:** We help woodworkers and makers build audiences as impressive as the things they build.

**Key differentiators:**
- Deep niche expertise — we understand woodworking, tools, and the maker community from the inside
- Full-service under one roof — video, social, and brand strategy so clients don't juggle multiple vendors
- Results-focused — content is tied to business outcomes (leads, sales, audience growth), not vanity metrics

---

## Business Model

- **Pricing:** Monthly retainer packages (tiered by service scope) plus project-based pricing for one-off productions
- **Revenue target:** Define per fiscal year based on active retainer count and project pipeline
- **Key metrics:** Client retention rate (north star), monthly content output per client, audience growth rate, inbound leads attributed to content

---

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Overlap | Our Advantage |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| General social media agencies | Social management and content calendars | We specialize in woodworking/maker content — no ramp-up on the niche |
| Freelance videographers | Video shooting and editing | We provide strategy and publishing, not just raw footage |
| DIY content tools (Canva, CapCut) | Self-service content creation | We free up the maker's time so they can stay in the shop |
