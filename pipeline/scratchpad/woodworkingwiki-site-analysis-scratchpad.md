# WoodworkingWiki.com Analysis — Scratchpad

## Synthesis Notes

### The Big Picture
WoodworkingWiki.com is a dead site. It was a small WordPress content project launched in February 2015 by a solo creator (Steve Squires), sporadically updated through April 2018, and abandoned entirely after that. It currently receives approximately 1,325 monthly visits — almost entirely from long-tail organic search queries — and has no active social, referral, or direct traffic.

### What makes this interesting for Bespoke Woodcraft Studio?
- The domain name "woodworkingwiki.com" is strong — memorable, keyword-rich, and implies authority
- The site demonstrates what happens when a content project launches without sustained commitment
- The claimed 122K Instagram followers vs. 1,325 monthly site visits is a cautionary tale about vanity metrics
- The niche (woodworking how-to content) is the same space we operate in — this is competitive intelligence

### Key Findings to Highlight in Study Guide

1. **Traffic: ~1,325 visits/month** — This is near-zero in content site terms. For context, a healthy niche blog gets 10K-100K/month.

2. **Update frequency: ZERO since April 2018** — The site has been dormant for 8 years. No new content, no maintenance, no community engagement.

3. **Content volume: 22 posts total** — That's not a wiki, that's barely a blog. The "wiki" branding overpromises dramatically.

4. **Publishing pattern was erratic:**
   - 9 posts in first 6 months (2015)
   - 22-month gap
   - 9 posts updated in one day (June 2017 — likely bulk republish)
   - 4 posts over 4 months (early 2018)
   - Nothing since

5. **Traffic is 100% organic search** — No one is coming directly, no one is sharing on social, no one is linking to it. Pure search tail.

6. **The Instagram anomaly** — 122K followers claimed but zero social traffic to the site. Either bought followers, a repurposed/sold account, or content that doesn't drive clicks.

7. **Solo operator who disappeared** — Steve Squires has no meaningful web presence beyond this site and a dormant Medium profile.
