## What We Do (Two Business Lines)

### 1. Custom Furniture

We design and build bespoke furniture from domestic and exotic hardwoods. Every piece starts with a free 30-minute design consultation and is built collaboratively with the client. We work in white oak, walnut, maple, cherry, ash, and exotic species. Typical timeline is 4-8 weeks. Every piece carries a lifetime warranty on joints and structural integrity.

**Key offerings:** Dining tables, dressers, cabinets, chairs, custom one-of-a-kind pieces, cabinetry solutions.

**Differentiator:** We work with you, not just for you. The design process is collaborative, not transactional.

### 2. Content Creation for Makers

We're a done-for-you content agency for professional woodworkers and makers. We handle video production, editing, content strategy, and distribution so makers can stay in the shop.

**How it works:**
- Starter Package: $2,500-$4,000 for a one-month trial (1-2 shoot days + editing + distribution)
- Monthly Retainer: $3,000-$5,000/month for ongoing content production
- Target annual client value: $36K-$60K per retained client

**Who we work with:** Professional woodworkers/makers doing $200K+ annual revenue with visually compelling work who are willing to be filmed and trust the creative process. Single decision-maker. Thinks in months, not days.

**Results:** 90%+ year-over-year client retention. 2-3x average subscriber growth in year one. Growth driven almost entirely by referrals within the woodworking community.

## Audience Context

Our audience falls into two groups:

**Furniture Clients:** Homeowners and businesses who want custom, one-of-a-kind furniture. They value quality over speed, personalization over mass production, and they want to be involved in the design process.

**Content Clients (Makers):** Professional woodworkers doing $200K+ who need help building an online audience. They're great at their craft but invisible online. They value authenticity, trust, and a partner who understands their world.

**Content Viewers:** People interested in woodworking, making, and craft. They value process over polish, honesty over hype, and they want to learn something real from someone who actually does the work. Average age 44, predominantly male (92%), with a growing younger and female audience on social platforms.
