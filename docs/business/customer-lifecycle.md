## Overview

A client's journey starts when they find us — usually through our own content, a referral from another maker, or a community event. From there, we qualify the fit, prove our value through a starter package, and transition into an ongoing retainer where content compounds month over month. The goal is a long-term creative partnership where we become an extension of their shop.

```
Discovery → Qualification Call → Starter Package → Onboarding & Retainer Kickoff → Ongoing Content Production → Renewal & Expansion
```

---

## Phase 1: Discovery & Qualification

**Goal:** Find makers who are a strong fit and filter out those who aren't — before anyone wastes time.

**Duration:** 1-2 weeks

**Key activities:**
- Inbound inquiry comes in (DM, website form, referral, event connection)
- Quick screening: Do they have an established business? Is the craft visual? Are they in a serviceable location?
- Schedule and conduct a 30-minute discovery call to assess ICP fit and content readiness

**Exit criteria:** Prospect scores 65+ on the ICP scoring system and expresses clear interest in a starter package.

**Owner:** Ahmed (founder) or lead producer

---

## Phase 2: Starter Package

**Goal:** Deliver a small, high-impact engagement that proves our value and builds trust on both sides.

**Duration:** 1 month (typically 1-2 shoot days plus editing and delivery)

**Key activities:**
- Pre-production planning: visit the shop (virtual or in-person), develop a shot list, agree on deliverables
- Shoot day(s): capture the maker at work, interview footage, detail shots of finished pieces
- Post-production: edit 2-4 videos (mix of long-form and short-form cuts), deliver for review

**Exit criteria:** Client has received final deliverables, is happy with the quality, and wants to discuss an ongoing retainer.

**Owner:** Lead producer (with Ahmed involved in creative direction)

---

## Phase 3: Onboarding & Retainer Kickoff

**Goal:** Transition from the one-off starter into a structured monthly retainer with clear expectations.

**Duration:** 1-2 weeks (overlaps with start of first retainer month)

**Key activities:**
- Retainer proposal: define monthly deliverables, shoot cadence, content calendar, and pricing
- Set up systems: shared folder for assets, content calendar, communication channel (Slack, text, or email — whatever the client prefers)
- Brand and voice alignment: document the client's story, key talking points, visual style, and any topics to avoid

**Exit criteria:** Signed retainer agreement, content calendar for the first quarter, and systems in place for smooth production.

**Owner:** Ahmed (relationship) and lead producer (operations)

---

## Phase 4: Ongoing Content Production

**Goal:** Deliver consistent, high-quality content each month that grows the client's audience and drives business results.

**Duration:** Ongoing (target 18-24+ months)

**Key activities:**
- Monthly shoot days at the client's shop (1-3 days depending on retainer tier)
- Edit and deliver agreed-upon videos, short-form content, and any additional assets
- Monthly check-in: review content performance, discuss upcoming projects or seasonal opportunities, adjust the content calendar

**Success metrics:** Content published on schedule, audience growth (followers, views, engagement), client satisfaction, and — over time — inbound leads or sales the client attributes to content.

**Owner:** Lead producer (day-to-day), Ahmed (quarterly reviews and relationship)

---

## Phase 5: Renewal & Expansion

**Goal:** Retain the client for another year and explore opportunities to deepen the partnership.

**Key activities:**
- Quarterly business review: show content performance trends, audience growth, and ROI indicators
- Propose expanded services: photography for their website, email newsletter content, product launch campaigns, or event coverage
- Referral ask: if the client is happy, ask for introductions to other makers in their network

**Owner:** Ahmed (strategic relationship and upsell conversations)

## Handoff Points

| From | To | What Gets Transferred | How |
|------|----|-----------------------|-----|
| Discovery (Ahmed) | Production (Lead Producer) | Client brief, ICP score, discovery call notes, starter scope | Shared doc + kickoff meeting |
| Starter Package (Lead Producer) | Retainer Onboarding (Ahmed + Producer) | Starter deliverables, client feedback, production notes, retainer proposal | Internal debrief + client transition call |

---

## Lifecycle Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Discovery-to-Starter Conversion | 40%+ | Measures whether we're attracting the right prospects |
| Starter-to-Retainer Conversion | 70%+ | Proves the starter package delivers enough value to earn ongoing trust |
| Average Retainer Duration | 18+ months | Content compounds — longer relationships mean better results for clients and better economics for us |
