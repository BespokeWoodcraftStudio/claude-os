## How to Use This Taxonomy

This is a **content planning tool**, not an encyclopedia. Each leaf-level topic should map to at least one content piece — a social post, a blog article, a how-to guide, or a video script. Use the 10 top-level categories to organize your editorial calendar, and the sub-levels to generate specific content ideas.

Section 10 ("Content Angles & Formats") is different from the rest — it provides **lenses** you can apply across any topic to generate specific content hooks.

**Audience:** Hobbyist woodworkers — weekend builders, people setting up their first shop, folks learning by doing.

## 1. Tools & Equipment

### 1.1 Hand Tools
- **Saws**: dovetail saws, coping saws, Japanese pull saws, backsaws, bow saws
- **Chisels & Gouges**: bench chisels, mortise chisels, paring chisels, carving gouges
- **Planes**: block planes, jack planes, smoothing planes, jointer planes, shoulder planes, spokeshaves
- **Layout & Measuring**: combination squares, marking gauges, marking knives, dividers, winding sticks, straightedges
- **Hammers & Mallets**: wooden mallets, dead-blow hammers, brass hammers
- **Clamps**: bar clamps, pipe clamps, F-clamps, spring clamps, hand screws, band clamps, toggle clamps
- **Sharpening**: whetstones, waterstones, diamond plates, honing guides, strops, grinding wheels

### 1.2 Power Tools
- **Saws**: table saws, miter saws, band saws, circular saws, jigsaws, scroll saws, track saws
- **Routers**: plunge routers, fixed-base routers, router tables, router bits (profiles, joinery, pattern)
- **Drills & Drivers**: cordless drills, drill presses, impact drivers, brad-point bits, Forstner bits
- **Sanders**: random orbital sanders, belt sanders, drum sanders, oscillating spindle sanders, detail sanders
- **Planers & Jointers**: benchtop planers, jointers, planer/jointer combos
- **Lathes**: mini lathes, midi lathes, full-size lathes, lathe chucks, lathe tools

### 1.3 Shop-Made Jigs & Fixtures
- Crosscut sleds, taper jigs, tenon jigs, box joint jigs, circle-cutting jigs
- Router jigs (dado, dovetail, flush-trim templates)
- Sharpening jigs, drill press jigs, clamping cauls

### 1.4 Workbenches & Work-Holding
- Bench styles (Roubo, Nicholson, split-top, Scandinavian)
- Vises (face vise, tail vise, leg vise, moxon vise)
- Bench dogs, holdfasts, planing stops, bench hooks

### 1.5 Tool Buying & Maintenance
- First tools to buy (starter kit recommendations)
- Used vs. new (finding deals, what to look for)
- Tool restoration (rust removal, handle replacement, tuning up old planes)
- Sharpening routines and schedules
- Battery platform decisions for cordless tools

## 2. Materials & Wood Science

### 2.1 Wood Species
- **Domestic Hardwoods**: oak (red/white), walnut, cherry, maple (hard/soft), ash, hickory, poplar

## 10. Content Angles & Formats

These are **lenses** you can apply to any topic above to generate specific content pieces.

### 10.1 Content Formats
- "How-to" guides (step-by-step tutorials)
- "What I learned" / reflection posts
- Tool reviews and comparisons
- Before/after transformations
- Mistake breakdowns ("What went wrong and how I fixed it")
- Time-lapses and process videos
- Quick tips (30-second format)
- Deep dives / long-form explainers
- "3 ways to do X" comparison posts
- Myth-busting posts

### 10.2 Content Hooks for Hobbyists
- "Your first [project/tool/joint]" — beginner entry points
- "Weekend project" — builds that fit a Saturday
- "Under $50 / $100" — budget-conscious builds
- "One tool, one board" — constraint-based projects
- "Common mistakes" — relatable learning moments
- "Skip this, do this instead" — upgrade advice
- "What nobody tells you about..." — insider knowledge
- Seasonal hooks (holiday gifts, spring outdoor builds, fall shop prep)

### 10.3 Series & Recurring Themes
- "Shop tip of the week"
- "Wood of the month" (species spotlights)
- "Tool Tuesday" or similar recurring features
- Beginner journey / learning series
- Build-along series (multi-part project walkthroughs)
- "Ask a woodworker" Q&A format
