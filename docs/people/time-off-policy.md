## Philosophy

We're a small team that does creative work, and creative work requires rest. We offer flexible PTO because we trust everyone here to manage their time like adults. The deal is simple: take the time you need, give the team a heads-up, and make sure your projects are covered.

---

## PTO Guidelines

We offer flexible PTO with no hard cap. We track time off informally to make sure everyone is actually taking breaks.

### Requesting Time Off

1. Drop a message in Slack to Ahmed and anyone you're collaborating with on active projects
2. For 1-2 days off, a few days' notice is enough. For a week or more, give at least 2 weeks' notice
3. Hand off any active client work or deadlines before you go — leave a note in the project channel so nothing falls through the cracks
4. Set your Slack status and update your calendar so the team knows you're away

### Expectations

- We encourage everyone to take at least 15 days off per year, not counting holidays. Burnout helps nobody
- Check with the team before planning time off around major client launches or deadlines
- If something comes up last minute (you're sick, life happens), just let us know as soon as you can — no guilt

## Other Leave Types

### Sick Leave

If you're sick, stay home and rest. No doctor's note required, no guilt. Just let the team know so we can cover anything urgent. Sick days don't count against your PTO.

### Parental Leave

New parents (birth, adoption, or foster placement) get up to 12 weeks of paid leave. We'll work with you on timing and transition — every family situation is different, and we want to support yours.

### Bereavement Leave

Take up to 5 paid days for the loss of an immediate family member, and up to 3 days for extended family or close friends. If you need more time, just talk to Ahmed.

### Jury Duty and Voting

We'll cover your regular pay for jury duty. On election days, take whatever time you need to vote — no questions asked.
